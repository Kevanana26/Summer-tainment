In 2021, I found a Group knwon as Saber Guild, and Ive been with them ever since then. Saber Guild is a non-profit international Star Wars costuming organization that specializes in choreographed lightsaber combat performances. They have multiple chapters in different countries and cities, and their members strive to promote the Star Wars fandom while supporting various charity organizations. Saber Guild members create their custom Jedi and Sith costumes, develop their unique character narrative, and train in different styles of stage combat using lightsabers. They perform at conventions, charity events, and other public and private gatherings, showcasing their skills and passion for the Star Wars universe.
